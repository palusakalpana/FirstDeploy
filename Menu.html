Morning Delights

Butter Croissant

A quintessential French pastry, the butter croissant is a delightful blend of flaky layers and buttery richness. Freshly baked to a golden brown, each bite offers a satisfying crispiness followed by a melt-in-your-mouth softness. Served warm with a dollop of whipped butter and a side of tangy strawberry jam, it’s the perfect start to the day, whether enjoyed with coffee or tea. see more

Avocado Toast

A healthy and flavorful option, avocado toast is made with ripe, creamy avocado mashed onto freshly toasted sourdough bread. Topped with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of chili flakes for a touch of heat, it’s garnished with fresh herbs like basil or parsley for an added burst of flavor. A light yet satisfying breakfast choice that’s both delicious and nourishing. see more

Breakfast Quiche

A slice of savory quiche offers a satisfying combination of buttery, flaky pastry and a rich, creamy filling. Made with eggs, cheese, spinach, and crispy bacon, the quiche is baked until golden and set to perfection. Served with a refreshing side salad, it’s a comforting yet elegant breakfast or brunch option. see more



Hearty Mains

Creamy Mushroom Risotto

This Italian classic features arborio rice cooked slowly to perfection in a creamy broth, creating a velvety texture. Sautéed mushrooms are stirred in, infusing the dish with their earthy flavors, while a generous helping of Parmesan cheese adds richness and depth. A sprinkle of fresh thyme or parsley on top gives the dish a burst of freshness, making it a perfect, comforting meal. see more

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A rich and indulgent dish, Chicken Alfredo combines tender pieces of chicken with creamy Alfredo sauce, which is made by simmering butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. The sauce envelops the fettuccine pasta, creating a luscious and satisfying dish. Garnished with a sprinkle of Parmesan and cracked black pepper, it’s the perfect blend of flavors for pasta lovers. see more

Grilled Vegetable Panini

A warm, crispy panini filled with grilled zucchini, bell peppers, and roasted tomatoes, offering a smoky, savory flavor. The vegetables are drizzled with fresh pesto sauce, which adds a burst of herbal richness. Sandwiched between toasted ciabatta bread, the panini is grilled to perfection, providing the perfect balance of crunchy exterior and tender interior. It's a healthy and filling choice for lunch or dinner. see more



Sides & Snacks

Garlic Bread

Freshly baked baguette slices are generously spread with a mixture of garlic, butter, and parsley, then toasted until golden and crispy. The result is a wonderfully aromatic, buttery treat that’s perfect for dipping into soups, salads, or enjoying on its own as a savory snack. see more

Bruschetta

A simple yet flavorful Italian appetizer, bruschetta is made by topping toasted bread with a mix of diced tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of balsamic reduction. The combination of the crunchy bread and the juicy, tangy topping makes for a refreshing bite, with the basil adding an herbaceous lift and the balsamic bringing sweetness and depth. see more

Stuffed Mushrooms

These bite-sized treats are made by hollowing out fresh mushrooms and filling them with a rich mixture of garlic, cream cheese, herbs, and Parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, they’re the perfect savory snack or appetizer, with a satisfying mix of creamy filling and tender mushroom caps. see more



Sweet Endings

Tiramisu

A luxurious Italian dessert made by layering espresso-soaked ladyfingers with rich mascarpone cream. The layers are dusted with cocoa powder, adding a bitter contrast to the sweet and creamy texture. Each bite is an indulgence, with the deep flavor of coffee and the smooth, velvety mascarpone cream balancing perfectly. see more

Chocolate Mousse

A decadent and velvety dessert, chocolate mousse is made with rich, melted chocolate and whipped cream, creating a light yet indulgent treat. The mousse is chilled to set, offering a smooth and airy texture that melts in your mouth. It’s topped with a dollop of whipped cream and a cherry for a sweet, satisfying finish to any meal. see more

Lemon Cheesecake

This creamy cheesecake has a refreshing lemon twist, with a tangy citrus flavor cutting through the rich, velvety cream cheese base. The crisp graham cracker crust adds a buttery crunch, while fresh berries or a lemon glaze on top offer an extra touch of sweetness and color. It’s a perfect dessert for those who love a balance of sweet and tart. see more



Beverages

Cafe Latte

A smooth and comforting beverage, the café latte combines espresso with steamed milk, creating a creamy texture that’s both rich and mellow. Topped with a layer of foam, it’s perfect for sipping and pairs beautifully with any of the breakfast or dessert items. see more

Iced Matcha Latte

A refreshing drink made with matcha green tea powder, which is whisked into a smooth, creamy blend of milk (dairy or non-dairy), and served over ice. The earthy bitterness of the matcha is balanced by the sweetness of the milk, making it a cooling, energizing drink. see more

Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice

Made from ripe, juicy oranges, this refreshing drink is packed with vitamin C. The natural sweetness and tanginess of the juice provide a zesty and invigorating way to start the day, perfect for pairing with a light breakfast or brunch. see more
